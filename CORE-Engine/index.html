Understanding Your Impact

What is a VITA Tax Refund?

VITA is a free IRS program helping families keep their full refund by avoiding high tax prep fees.

What is CORE?

CORE is a data-driven model used in Snohomish County to help families move from crisis to long-term stability.

Why calculate this?

We show how a one-time refund bridges the "Self-Sufficiency Gap" for essential costs like rent in our community.

Data Methodology: The $120,000 annual benchmark ($10,000/mo) is based on the Self-Sufficiency Standard for Washington State (Snohomish County) for a family with two adults and two school-aged children.
